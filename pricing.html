Membership FAQs

Find answers to common questions about our membership plans and policies.

Yes, you can change your plan at any time. If you upgrade, the new rate will be prorated for the remainder of your billing cycle. If you downgrade, the change will take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.

There is no cancellation fee for monthly memberships. For annual memberships, cancellation within the first 30 days incurs no fee. After 30 days, a prorated refund will be provided based on the time remaining in your annual commitment.

Yes, we offer corporate wellness programs and family discounts. Corporate plans provide 15% off for groups of 5 or more employees. Family plans offer 10% off for additional family members. Contact our membership team for details.

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), debit cards, and digital payments through UPI. We also offer the option for automatic monthly billing for your convenience.

Yes, we offer a 3-day free trial for new members. This includes access to all equipment and one group class of your choice. Schedule your trial by contacting us or visiting the studio.
